We are CMYK.

We are the future of multisport giving back

10.23.19

We are in a class of our own. Maybe we are age groupers, weekend warriors, first timers, millionth timers...we crave doing what it takes to go to our next level. We are CMYK.

Often it crosses our mind that we are lucky to have the opportunity to achieve our goals, but not everyone has the resources to do what we can do. CMYK athletes will continue to push their limits while simultaneously reaching out to the community to help those in need.

Follow us as we start our journey. Join us to enhance yours.

CMYK Inaugural Campaign to benefit Open Door Mission Rochester!

02.10.20

CMYK is kicking off our mission with an inaugural 4 week campaign to benefit the Open Door Mission - Rochester!

Open Door Mission is looking for food/maintenance/hygiene/clothing in efforts to restore hope and lives of impoverished men, women, and children of Rochester.

In true "Craving Multisport, Yielding Kindness" fashion, a small group of CMYK athletes is taking on a "4-4-48 Challenge" near the end of the campaign. This challenge involves running 4 miles, every 4 hours, for a total of 48 hours. We will be running around the Rochester area, including the Freezeroo White House Challenge! The multisport community is privileged with the opportunity to swim/bike/run in any temperature hot or cold, but some in our own backyard must brave the elements without any choice...we aim to try and change that.

Open Door Mission Drop Off Information 156 N. Plymouth Ave Rochester NY 14608 Drop off any time from 9 am to 5 pm Monday through Friday

GRTC Freezeroo Drop Off Location: 02/22/2020 - 1100 Lake Rd, Webster, NY 14580 Rochester Running Company will serve as a drop off location during regular business hours: 1387 Mt Hope Ave, Rochester, NY 14620
